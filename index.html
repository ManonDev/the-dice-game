The Dice Games

The dice game is played with 2 players.

Each player has a temporary score (CURRENT) and an overall score.

On each turn, the player has his CURRENT initialized to 0 and can roll a dice as many times as he wishes. The result of a throw is added to the CURRENT.

During his turn, the player can decide at any time to:

- Click on the "Hold" option, which sends the points of the CURRENT to the GLOBAL. It will then be the other player's turn.

- Roll the dice. If he rolls a 1, his CURRENT score is lost and his turn is over.

The first player to reach 100 points overall wins the game !
